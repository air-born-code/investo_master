## Executive signal

This first edition commissions the Investo Master publishing system. It contains no security recommendation and no fabricated market analysis. Its purpose is to prove that a dated research record can become a polished HTML report, a readable plain-text fallback, a permanent historical article, and an idempotent Gmail delivery.

## What changed this week

1. The core long-term research mandate was established.
2. Weekly research and extremely infrequent buying were separated explicitly.
3. A thirteen-gate conviction policy was adopted.
4. A relational CSV research architecture was designed.
5. GitHub became the project's durable source and future article archive.

## Decision posture

No asset is under decision review. That is the correct outcome for Issue 000. Investo Master will not manufacture urgency or lower its standards merely because a weekly report is due.

## The operating system

```text
Discover → Watch → Research → High Conviction → Wait for Price → Human Decision
              ↘ Reject                 ↘ Thesis Broken
```

High conviction is a research state, not an instruction to buy. An investment becomes reviewable only after the thesis, primary evidence, variant perception, durability, management, economics, bear case, valuation, pre-mortem, kill criteria, monitoring plan, and separate position-sizing assessment are complete.
